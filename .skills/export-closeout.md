# Export Closeout Skill

Use this workflow at the end of a major session, feature, import, audit, or drafting pass.

## Purpose

Make sure useful work is preserved, exportable, and easy to resume.

## Inputs

Use what is available:

- changed files
- feature packet
- canon candidates
- source/claim updates
- visual assets or prompts
- validation/index output
- unresolved markers
- creator preferences from `project/export.md`

## Closeout Shape

```markdown
Closeout:

- Changed:
- Preserved artifacts:
- Validation:
- Open points:
- Recommended next actions:
```

Keep it concise. Link or name files when useful.

## Export Checks

- Does the creator need Markdown, table, prose, hybrid, YAML, JSON, visual prompt pack, or game/tool-ready output?
- Are source/provenance notes included where needed?
- Are draft/candidate/approved statuses clear?
- Are unresolved decisions visible?
- Should the creator commit, export, copy, or archive anything before stopping?

## Rules

- Do not invent completion that did not happen.
- Do not dump huge summaries unless requested.
- Do not transform private or sensitive material into a public-ready export without confirmation.
- Keep forward prompts active unless the creator asked for no prompts.
