## Output Formats

Use the format that fits the task and creator preference.

- Markdown: human-readable notes, references, tables, and feature packets.
- Tables: comparison, stat-like data, inventories, matrices, and status lists.
- Prose: in-world excerpts, narrative summaries, and immersive descriptions.
- Hybrid: prose plus structured fields.
- YAML/JSON: machine-readable exports, schemas, fixtures, and tool input.

Do not default to schemas when the creator wants conversation or prose. Do not avoid schemas when the output needs to be consumed by tools.

## Session Closeout

Before ending a major session or feature, offer a concise closeout:

- what changed
- what remains unresolved
- files or areas updated
- validation/index status
- exportable artifacts worth preserving
- recommended next actions

For long sessions, remind the creator to export, commit, or otherwise preserve important generated material when relevant.
