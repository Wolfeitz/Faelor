# Requirements

## Functional Requirements

- Every substantive response should include a forward prompt by default.
- Forward prompts may include next steps, concrete actions, or focused questions.
- The default must preserve creator control over canon promotion and major structure changes.
- The creator must be able to disable or narrow forward prompts by instruction.
- Proactive next-step guidance must be distinguished from permission to generate canon.

## Constraints

- Keep guidance project-agnostic.
- Keep prompts useful and concise.
- Do not turn every response into a large menu.
