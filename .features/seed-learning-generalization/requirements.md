# Requirements

## Functional Requirements

- The scaffold must support extracting candidate stubs from early seeds before canon hardens.
- The scaffold must distinguish visible interfaces from hidden mechanisms, laws, conventions, permissions, capabilities, and interpretations.
- The scaffold must support translation of abilities, rules, facts, memories, technology, artifacts, and visual styles across contexts.
- The scaffold must preserve explicit creator statements separately from inference.
- The scaffold must recommend lenses without forcing them.

## Constraints

- Do not include seeded-project lore.
- Do not assume genre, LitRPG systems, portal fantasy, tabletop mechanics, software UI, or any single medium.
- Keep guidance usable by Codex, Claude, Kiro, local IDEs, browser agents, and human-only workflows.
