# Project Foundation

Status: candidate

## Purpose

Establish the working frame for Fealor before broad canon drafting.

## Working Frame

- Built for: worldbuilding exercise first, with possible novel use later.
- Ruleset/source base: original LitRPG/system fantasy; no predefined tabletop or source-canon ruleset.
- Authorship mode: collaborative full generation. Use assisted drafting by default, with scoped full generation when the creator grants authority for a bounded element.
- Tone: cosmic mystery, System fantasy, interface literacy, reincarnation weirdness, practical craft, and absurd-but-logical skill translation.
- Primary creative tension: people were right that the moon is artificial and aliens are aware of Earth, but wrong about why.

## Seed Premise

The moon is an artificial component of a local consciousness-gathering array. At death, human consciousness is intercepted and offered a choice:

- proceed naturally into whatever comes next, which even the System does not fully explain; or
- defer that passage by entering The System.

The System hosts or connects countless scenario worlds. These may function partly as intergalactic entertainment, partly as afterlife deferral, partly as population management, and partly as something older or stranger. Entrants from many worlds can choose scenarios including tech worlds, mundane Earth-like worlds, fantasy worlds, and other rule environments.

Earth may itself be one such scenario, possibly with special rules around memory suppression and ordinary-life continuity.

## First Foundation Questions

- How much of Earth is a scenario, and how much is a physical induction world?
- Who built or maintains the moon array?
- Are the "aliens" operators, viewers, sponsors, administrators, former entrants, or something else?
- Why are modern Earthborn only starting to trickle into the target fantasy world now?
- What is the target world's local relationship to gods, magic, classes, and The System?
