# Brief

## Purpose

Run a Provocateur extraction pass across Fealor's saved files and kickoff conversation to identify candidate things, implied infrastructure, missing decisions, and deeper inquiry paths.

## Scope

- Extract entities, systems, roles, skills, jobs, classes, tools, institutions, interfaces, and story mechanisms.
- Separate known material from inferred material.
- Flag items needing creator review.
- Preserve lore discussed in conversation even if it has not yet been promoted to canon.

## Non-Goals

- Do not approve canon.
- Do not resolve every open issue.
- Do not expand every stub into full worldbuilding yet.
