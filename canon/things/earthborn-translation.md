# Earthborn Translation

Status: candidate canon
Provenance: generated from creator discussion.

## Principle

The System converts prior experience by shape, not by human intent.

It does not ask whether an activity was a vocation, pastime, game, ritual, hobby, craft, profession, or training curriculum. It asks what repeatable competencies the consciousness reinforced, then maps those patterns into compatible local skills, traits, professions, classes, titles, or locked experience.

Core line:

> The System is not wrong. It just does not care whether Earth called it a game.

## Modern Earthborn

Modern Earthborn are beginning to trickle into a new magic-fantasy world. They are not unique, but they are rare enough that their odd translation profiles have not yet become common knowledge.

Earthborn tend to be over-indexed in:

- interface literacy;
- adaptive pattern recognition;
- abstracted combat and tool use;
- broad hobby exposure;
- media and genre literacy;
- systems thinking;
- feedback-loop training.

They tend to be under-indexed in:

- lived violence;
- pain, fear, blood, exhaustion, and consequence;
- local language and culture;
- actual magical embodiment;
- political and religious context.

## Classes And Professions

A class is not what a person is. It is the growth-vector the System applies to what that person does.

Skills can be learned by anyone. Classes decide which skills resonate, combine, discount, evolve, or unlock higher expressions.

Same skill, different class-vector:

- Brewing as Tavernkeeper: hospitality, rumor networks, morale, commerce.
- Brewing as Alchemist: reagents, potions, transformations.
- Brewing as Priest: offerings, rites, sacred intoxication.
- Brewing as Swarm Druid: honey, hive spirits, pollination, seasonal cycles.
- Brewing as Poisoner: toxins, dosage, masking, contamination.
- Brewing as Bard: performance, revelry, emotional resonance.

## Locked Experience

Locked experience is recognized prior-layer experience whose unlock criteria have not been met in the current scenario.

It is not necessarily false, rejected, or incompatible forever.

Possible locked clusters for the MC:

- World Administration.
- System Architecture.
- Agent Orchestration.
- Worldbuilding / Canon Design.
- Administrative Command.
- Entity Provisioning.
- Rule Arbitration.
- World Editing.

User-facing status:

```text
Locked Experience Cluster: World Administration
Unlock Criteria: Unknown
Integration: Deferred
Residual Effects: Passive only
```

Locked experience may still create pressure:

- interface affinity;
- command-language recognition;
- rule-boundary sensitivity;
- strange dreams;
- hidden prerequisites;
- unusual title resonance;
- suspicion from gods, administrators, or System watchers.
