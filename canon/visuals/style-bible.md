## Style Scope And Inheritance

Use scoped style rules when different parts of the project share a global style but need local variation.

Inheritance order, from broad to narrow:

1. Global project style
2. Medium or publication format
3. Nexus
4. Realm/world
5. Era or timeline state
6. Race/species/culture
7. Faction/guild/institution
8. System/UI/message layer
9. Scene, sequence, page, or asset-specific override

When rules conflict, the narrower rule usually wins unless a broader rule is marked `locked`.
